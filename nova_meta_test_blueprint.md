THE DIAGNOSTIC (read daily, from skill)
=================================================================
- SPENDS but NO sales/sub: fix the OFFER or LANDING. Check: is $9.99 first month
  obvious? Is the bridge LP loading fast? Is the Fanvue page convincing? (Usually the
  fix is LP/offer, not the ad.)
- NO SPEND: creative or audience mismatch. Swap creative (try a different winner) or
  broaden audience. Don't raise budget to force spend.
- SPENDS + sales: record ROAS. Double budget ONLY after 3+ consecutive days positive.
