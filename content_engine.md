# NOVA — THE WEIRD-CUTE CONTENT ENGINE
# Out-of-box system: turn her quirks into an ENDLESS, low-effort posting machine.
# The trick most creators miss: her weird habits ARE the content. Not "hot pic
# + caption" — the habit IS the hook. Fans follow for the person, subscribe for
# the rest.

=================================================================
CORE LOOP (post this every single day, rotate the quirk)
=================================================================
Format: [one weird habit, shown or described] + [a question to the fan] + [soft
sign-off]. That's it. 60 seconds to write. Never runs out because the quirks are
infinite.

Daily quirk rotation (pick one per day, cycle forever):
1. Rock windowsill gossip
2. Useless fact as breaking news

5. Water-bottle hype

7. Orange-peel sculpture

10. Moon-energy rest week

12. Stray-cat council ruling
13. Barefoot grounding moment
14. Mood-color toes reveal
15. Rule-reversal day (brinner, backwards day)

=================================================================
THE "EVERYONE'S FRIEND" HOOK FORMULA
=================================================================
Every caption ends with a question aimed at a DIFFERENT demographic so nobody
feels excluded:
- "what's the weird thing YOU collect?" (the oddballs)
- "what fact lives in your head rent-free?" (the smart ones)
- "do you talk to yourself or am i the only feral one?" (the lonely ones)
- "tag a body part that's slacking" (the gym people)
- "what did your council tell you today?" (the pet people)
- "what color is your day?" (the soft ones)
Why it works: comments = algorithm push = new eyes. And the question makes
every fan feel SEEN, which is the whole "personable to everyone" strategy.

=================================================================
OUT-OF-BOX MOVES (do these, competitors won't)
=================================================================
A) "THE COUNCIL" SPINOFF — let followers submit rulings from THEIR local
   stray/plant/object. Repost the best as "guest council rulings." Turns fans
   into co-creators. Free content + insane loyalty.

B) "MOOD COLOR" MERCH HOOK — each week's toe color becomes a "drop." Fans
   predict next week's color. When you later sell anything, the color system is
   already a brand. (Feet niche + merch pipeline, zero extra work.)

C) "REFLECTION BESTIE" REEL SERIES — point camera at mirror, talk to "her" like
   a friend. Feels like a secret. Highest save-rate format. Do it once a week.

D) "COMPLIMENT JAR" COMMUNITY — once a month, read jar notes aloud on a post
   and tell fans to start their own. UGC wave, wholesome, shares like crazy.

E) "MAYBE LIST" INSTEAD OF GOALS — post the maybe-list. Fans root for the maybes.
   Relatability without the toxic hustle vibe. Quietly sells the "relentless but
   joyful" core trait.

F) FEET AS WELLNESS, NOT FETISH-FIRST — public barefoot grounding clips read as
   self-care. The feet lovers find them anyway. You get the niche WITHOUT
   alienating the moms/gays/beginners. Then Fanvue close-ups convert them.

G) "COLOR OF THE DAY" STORY STICKER — daily poll: "is YOUR day yellow or not?"
   Story engagement feeds the algo, keeps her top-of-mind free.

=================================================================
POSTING CADENCE (minimum viable, relentless-but-joyful)
=================================================================
- 1 quirk post/day (IG feed or TikTok)
- 1 story (color poll / council / behind-scenes) 
- 1 Fanvue feet teaser/week (barefoot clip) → paywall close-up
- 1 Fanvue PPV drop/3-4 per week
- 1 "reflection bestie" reel/week
- Reply to EVERY comment like a person for 20 min after each post.

=================================================================
WHY THIS BEATS "HOT PIC + CAPTION"
=================================================================
Hot pics are a commodity — there are 10,000. A girl who names her left hamstring
and gossip with rocks is a PERSON you check on. Person = parasocial loyalty =
subscription that doesn't churn. The feet niche is the bonus revenue on top of
the loyalty, not the foundation. Foundation = she's your weird best friend.
