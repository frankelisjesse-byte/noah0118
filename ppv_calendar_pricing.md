# NOVA — PPV DROP CALENDAR + PRICING LADDER
# The heat tier makes most of its money ABOVE the sub. This file tells you WHAT
# to drop, WHEN, and at WHAT PRICE. Heat visuals are produced OFF-chat (image
# tool is SFW-only) — this file schedules the RELEASES and prices, you supply the
# assets. Cadence is the product. Consistency = revenue.

=================================================================
THE LADDER (every sub climbs this, one rung at a time)
=================================================================
RUNG 1 — FREE WALL POST (daily): teaser/curiosity. No paywall. Keeps them hooked.
RUNG 2 — CHEAP PPV ($6-8): easy yes. "try me" content. High volume, low friction.
RUNG 3 — MID PPV ($10-15): the meat. 3-4/week. This is the money.
RUNG 4 — PREMIUM PPV / SET ($18-30): bundles, themed drops, "full session" packs.
RUNG 5 — CUSTOM ($60+): they direct, you produce. Highest margin, slowest.

RULE: never drop Run 4-5 without first teasing it free (Run 1). The free post is
the trailer. The PPV is the movie.

=================================================================
WEEKLY PPV CADENCE (repeat forever, vary the theme)
=================================================================
MON — Cheap PPV ($6): "warm-up" tease set. Easy yes to start the week.
WED — Mid PPV ($12): the main drop. Themed (see rotation below).
FRI — Mid PPV ($14): weekend-energy drop. Bolder.
SUN — Premium SET ($20-25): "weekend pack" — 3 clips bundled, better value than
       buying separate. Drives the biggest single checkout.

= 4 paid drops/week. Plus daily free wall posts. Plus tips running always.

=================================================================
THEME ROTATION (keep it fresh, never random)
=================================================================
Rotate so regulars always get something new but know the rhythm:
WK1 — "barefoot & grounded" (feet niche lead — your differentiator)
WK2 — "stretch, no filter" (soft/implied, athletic)
WK3 — "the dangerous mind" (boldest tier, tease hard)
WK4 — "request week" (prompt subs for custom ideas, then sell the best as PPV)

Then loop. The feet niche (WK1) is your moat — lead with it monthly.

=================================================================
PRICING LADDER — EXACT NUMBERS
=================================================================
First 50 subs (intro lock-in): all PPV at the LOW end ($6 / $10 / $18).
Build the buy habit cheap, THEN raise.
After 50 subs: standard ($8 / $12-14 / $20-25).
Loyal inner circle (tagged, see onboarding): 20% off PPV as a retention gift.
Bundles: 3-clip pack = price of 2.5 (e.g. 3x$12 = $36, bundle $28). Always bundle.
Custom: base $60, +$20 per added element/specific request, rush +$30.

TIP MENU (live always): $5 / $10 / $25 buttons. Plus "fund my next set" post.
Custom CTA: once/week — "tell me your fantasy, i'll price it."

=================================================================
[ ] Free teaser posted 24-48h before (Run 1 trailer)
[ ] Caption: what it is + "full version locked 🔓" + price stated
[ ] DM the 5 most-active subs personally ("dropped the one i mentioned 👀")
[ ] 24h later: soft bump post ("still up, last few hours at this price")
[ ] Track buy rate in kpi_scorecard (target 20-40% of subs per mid drop)
[ ] Top buyer angle this week → make 2 more like it next week (WIN pattern)

=================================================================
THE "DO NOT" LIST
=================================================================
- Don't dump 5 PPV in one day. Cadence dies, fans feel milked, churn spikes.
- Don't skip the free teaser. Unsolicited paywall = low buy rate.
- Don't raise prices before the buy habit exists (first 50 subs = cheap).
- Don't let a week pass with zero PPV. The heat tier is why they stay subscribed.
- Don't promise a custom you can't deliver off-chat. Set timeline honestly.
