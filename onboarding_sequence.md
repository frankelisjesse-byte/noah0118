# NOVA — FIRST 10 SUBS ONBOARDING SEQUENCE (Day 0-3 per new sub)
# The relationship = the retention. A new sub who gets a real reply in the first
# 10 minutes stays. One who gets silence churns. This is the exact DM flow for
# each of your first 10 subs (then it scales — see "after 10" at bottom).
# Voice: lowercase, intimate, curious about THEM, never robotic.

=================================================================
DAY 0 — THE MOMENT THEY SUBSCRIBE
=================================================================
AUTO-REPLY (instant, Fanvue setting — already in fanvue_setup_sheet.md):
"hi baby, you made it 😏 i'm nova — the version of you don't get on the public
page. daily drops, PPV heat, and i actually reply. tell me what you came for and
i'll show you where to look. welcome to the dangerous side. 🤍"

THEN (you, within 10 min if possible): a PERSONALIZED follow-up. Pull one detail
if they said anything, else use this opener:
"ok real talk — thank you for subscribing, that's not nothing. before i show you
the good stuff, tell me: are you here for the wellness chaos, the heat, or just
to have a friend who texts back? (all three is a valid answer 😏)"
-> The question does two things: makes them feel SEEN, and tells YOU which upsell
   angle to use later.

=================================================================
DAY 1 — THE "GOOD MORNING" + PULL
=================================================================
DM (morning): "good morning 🤍 woke up, stretched, talked to my plant chad about
you. he says you're welcome here. what's the first thing YOU do when you wake up,
before the world gets loud?"
-> casual, invites a reply. If they answer, you've got a thread going.
ACTION: if they haven't opened the wall yet, drop:
"ps — the barefoot & grounded collection is up. the anklet's on, obviously. start
there if you're not sure where to look 👣"

=================================================================
DAY 2 — THE FIRST PPV TEASE (soft upsell)
=================================================================
DM (evening): "tonight's drop is... let's call it 'stretch with no plan, no
filter.' teaser's free on the wall, the rest is locked. peek at the preview — you
tell me if it's your kind of danger 😏"
-> Lets them self-select into the PPV. No pressure = higher buy rate.
IF they buy: "ok you've got GOOD taste, that's the one i was nervous about. more
like that coming. anything you want to see next?"
IF they don't: no chase. Just "no worries, the free drops keep coming daily 🤍"

=================================================================
DAY 3 — THE LOYALTY SEED
=================================================================
DM (any time): "real one question — day 3, you're still here, i appreciate that
more than you know. what would make this page worth staying for? i build it
around the people who actually talk to me. so tell me. 👀"
-> Turns a subscriber into a CO-CREATOR. They'll tell you exactly what to sell.
ACTION: offer the tip menu softly:
"also — if a drop ever makes you go 'damn,' there's a tip button. no pressure,
but it tells me what to make more of. 🤍"

=================================================================
THE 4 REPLY TONES (match to the sub)
=================================================================
THE QUIET ONE: ask 2 follow-up questions, never let it drop. They're watching.
THE CHATTY ONE: match energy, use their name, joke back. They'll promote you.
THE TIPPER: thank specifically ("the $25? you didn't have to — ok you did, thank
you 🤍"). Then ask what they want next — they'll tell you.
THE CUSTOM REQUESTER: "tell me exactly what you're picturing, i'll price it.
custom starts at $60." (script in dm_scripts.md)

=================================================================
AFTER YOUR FIRST 10 SUBS — SCALE IT
=================================================================
- Days 0-3 flow becomes your TEMPLATE. Clone per new sub.
- Use the caption_generator.py to stay ahead on wall content so DMs aren't rushed.
- Batch: every Sunday, DM your 5 most-active subs a personal "how's your week"
  to keep churn near zero (STALL C fix in kpi_scorecard_30day.md).
- Once you hit 20 subs, add a "loyal inner circle" tag — those get first look at
  PPV + a free gift monthly. That's your retention moat.

=================================================================
RED LINE
=================================================================
If any new sub goes 48h with NO human reply from you, you've already lost them.
The auto-reply buys the first 10 minutes — YOU have to show up in the first day.
Set a daily 20-min "DM hour" and protect it like a workout.
