6. INSTAGRAM MASTERY (mechanics that matter in 2026)
=================================================================
SIGNALS (what the algo rewards, in order):
  1. Watch time / completion rate on Reels (finish = strongest signal).
  2. Saves + shares (weighted ABOVE likes — they signal value).
  3. Comments (esp. conversation-starting ones — our question-close drives this).
  4. Follows from the post (new follower from content = gold).
  5. Dwell time on profile after.
TACTICS:
  - Reels first. Static gets ~10x less reach. We lead with video.
  - Hook in 1–2 sec, captions ON screen (most watch muted).
  - Loop the video seamlessly (watch again = more watch time).
  - First comment = the question + a CTA to the link in bio.
  - Post 1 Reel + 1–2 stories daily. Stories = the DM/funnel warm-up.
  - SEO in caption (keywords people SEARCH: "home mobility," "feet care").
  - Hashtags: 3–5 specific > 30 spammy. Niche > broad.
  - Carousels for "saves" content (how-to, routines) — the save-engine.
  - Reply to EVERY comment in first 60 min (signal boost).
  - Collab tags / repost-to-Reels to borrow audiences.

7. TIKTOK MASTERY (mechanics that matter in 2026)
=================================================================
SIGNALS:
  1. Completion rate + rewatches (loop = king).
  2. Shares (TikTok pushes shared content HARD).
  3. Comments + video replies (reply-to-comment videos = explosive).
  4. Follows from video.
  5. Watch-through to the very end (even 1 extra sec matters).
TACTICS:
  - Post 2–3x/day if possible (TikTok rewards volume + recency).
  - Hook in first SECOND (text-on-screen pattern interrupt).
  - Ride trends within 24–48h (sounds, formats, challenges) — but keep NOVA in
    character (our weird-cute spin on the trend = memorable).
  - Series formats ("POV:", "day X of...") build binge-watch retention.
  - Use the "she remembers you" angle in comments → reply videos.
  - Link in bio + a pinned comment pointing there (no off-platform links in cap).
  - Duet/stitch is forbidden for most, but we can stitch TRENDS (not people).
  - Keep videos 8–21s for max completion early; 30–45s once we have a base.
