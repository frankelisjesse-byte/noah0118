BANK ACCOUNT WITH NO PHOTO ID — the cheat sheet
================================================
Goal: a safe place to CATCH and HOLD the ~$7,500 settlement that does
NOT require a photo ID to open. Cash App is the backup catcher's mitt;
these are better long-term homes for the money.

to start with.

WHAT BANKS ACTUALLY CHECK (why no-ID is possible):
  By law banks must verify WHO you are ("Know Your Customer"). They can
  do this with your NAME + DATE OF BIRTH + SSN (or ITIN) + ADDRESS —
  a photo ID is the EASIEST way but not the ONLY way. Online/app banks
  often verify by matching your SSN + info to records, no photo needed.
  If one asks for a photo ID later, you move to the next on the list.

=====================================================================
BEST NO-PHOTO-ID OPTIONS (try in this order)
=====================================================================

1. CHIME  (app bank — usually SSN + info, often no photo ID up front)
   - Download Chime, sign up with name, DOB, SSN, address.
   - Gives you a routing + account number for direct deposit fast.
   - Good for catching the settlement, then spending/saving.
   - If they later ask for ID to "verify," you can often upload other
     docs (SS card, benefits letter). Ask their support.

2. VARO  (app bank — similar, SSN-based signup)
   - Same idea as Chime. Fast account + routing number.
   - Try this if Chime asks for a photo ID you don't have yet.

3. GO2BANK  (from Green Dot — app + at Walmart)
   - Can often open online with SSN. Also sold as a card at Walmart.

4. CASH APP  (you already have it — the BACKUP catcher)
   - Verify it (name/DOB/last-4 SSN, usually no photo). Limit up to
     $25k verified — your $7,500 fits. Use if the banks stall.

5. CREDIT UNION near you  (the sleeper — most flexible on ID)
   - Small local credit unions will often open an account with:
     Social Security card + a benefits award letter + a piece of mail
     with your address. Call one and ASK:
     "I don't have a current photo ID yet. Can I open an account with
      my Social Security card and my SSD benefits letter?"
   - Credit unions WANT members and bend more than big banks.

=====================================================================
THE SMART MONEY MOVE (once the settlement lands)
=====================================================================
  - CATCH it in Chime/Varo/Cash App (whatever opens first + safely).
  - Do NOT leave all $7,500 sitting in a spend app. Move most of it to
    a SAVINGS account (Chime and Varo both have one) so it's not easy
    to burn and is your MOVE-IN WAR CHEST for the next place.
  - This money's job: first+last+deposit on a home + a cushion. Guard
    it like the roof it will become.

=====================================================================
IF EVERY APP ASKS FOR A PHOTO ID
=====================================================================
  Then the ID becomes step one (THE_MAP Box 2). Fastest ID paths:
  - Call 211: "I need help getting a state ID, I'm losing my housing."
    Many cities get you one FREE and fast.
  - SSA benefits letter + SS card often opens a credit union in person.
  - A paper CHECK from the settlement (ask the lawyer) can wait safely
    until the ID is in hand — a check doesn't expire fast and can't be
    frozen by an app.
