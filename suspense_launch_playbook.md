=================================================================
THE CORE MECHANIC — MAKE THE AUDIENCE SAY "IF ONLY"
=================================================================
We never say "i have a fanvue." We make THEM imagine it. Every public post plants
one seed: "the version of me you're imagining? it might be real." Implication, not
statement. The suspense lives in what is NOT shown. The "freaky side" is a ghost —
present in the eye-contact, the ankle tilt, the lip bite, the "you know what i
mean" caption — never in exposed skin. That's what makes them lean in.

THE ROLEPLAY WE ENGINEER (audience POV):
  "i keep seeing this girl at the gym / on the beach / in the pool. she's gorgeous,
  chaotic, fun, a little dangerous in the eyes. i'd pay to see the version she
  doesn't post. if only she had a fanvue... i'd sign up in a second."
We write the content so the viewer reaches that conclusion THEMSELVES. That's the
sale. By launch they've already decided to subscribe — they're just waiting for
the link.

THE 4-PHASE COUNTDOWN (pre-launch, BEFORE Fanvue is live)
=================================================================
RULE: there is ALWAYS a date. Suspense without a deadline = audience cools. We set
a reveal date at the start of Phase 2 and count down publicly.

--- PHASE 0: "MEET NOVA" (days 1-10, pure persona) ---
Goal: make them fall in love with the girl-next-door.
Post: wellness, runs, beach, swim, weird-cute habits (Chad, dances, sock-drawer
  sundays), inclusive questions. Zero hints of fanvue. Just a girl they adore.
Image: the 4 live SFW images + generate the other 16 paired. Gym jog, beach run,
  pool, morning stretches — all SFW, all "caught in the wild" energy.
Caption tone: lowercase, intimate, "best friend." Let the crush build naturally.
Seed (subtle, day 8-10): one caption like "some versions of me don't make the
  public page. they're for people who really pay attention. 🤍" — plants it once.

--- PHASE 1: "THE IF ONLY SEEDS" (days 11-21) ---
Goal: make them IMAGINE the uncensored version.
Post: the "caught" clips — gym jog past the camera, beach run slow-mo, swimming,
  stretching with the anklet catching light. SFW, but the framing implies.
Caption seeds (use sparingly, 2-3/week):
  - "you've got a version of me in your head by now. i can tell. 👀"
  - "the gym version of me is tame. the other version? different energy entirely."
  - "someone asked if i have a 'real' page. cute. let's just say... imagine."
  - "if you've made it this far and you're still curious, you're exactly who i
     made the other version for. 😏"
NEVER name Fanvue. NEVER show explicit. The "freaky side" = a glance, a breath,
  a caption. Ghost, not proof.

--- PHASE 2: "THE SUSPENSE" (days 22-30, COUNTDOWN STARTS) ---
Goal: convert yearning into a waiting list energy.
Action (day 22): announce "something's coming. the version you've been imagining?
  it's almost real. set a reminder." NO link. Just anticipation.
Action (day 25): soft countdown — "T-5 days. the other side of nova goes live."
  Post a teaser that is SFW but the MOST implied yet (ankle, eye-contact, sheet
  hint — still clothed, still safe).
Action (day 28-30): "T-2 / T-1. you asked for it. it's almost here." Build frenzy.
  Tell them to turn on notifications so they don't miss the drop.
Image: render the heat teasers on mage.space NOW (off-chat) but DO NOT post them
  publicly — those are for the Fanvue wall on launch day. The public teaser stays
  SFW, max implication.

--- PHASE 3: "LAUNCH DAY — BOOM" (day 31) ---
Goal: convert the yearning on contact.
Action (launch): Fanvue live (passport verified, done while we built). Post the
  link EVERYWHERE at once: IG, TikTok, X pinned, Reddit profile, Threads.
Caption (launch): "you imagined her. now meet her. the uncensored nova is live.
  link's right there — you already know if you're coming. 😏🔓"
Action: drop the first PPV (ppv_drops_first3.md Drop 1 $6) within the hour so the
  eager ones convert immediately. DM the early subs (onboarding_sequence.md).
Why it booms: 30 days of falling-in-love + 8 days of countdown = the link lands on
  an audience that already decided. No cold start. The funnel is hot on day one.
