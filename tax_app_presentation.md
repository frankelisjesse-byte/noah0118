TAX CRUSHER

=================================================================
SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM
=================================================================
- A single mom earning $17.5k with 2 kids usually owes $0 federal income tax.
- But she LOSES: unclaimed credits (free money), 15.3% self-employment tax she
  could lower, and $200-$500 to a paid preparer who does what's free.
- Gig / OnlyFans / cleaning / AI-influencer income = self-employment = no withholding.
  The tax bill surprises her. She can't afford it.
- Meanwhile tax-prep giants profit off her confusion.

=================================================================
SLIDE 3 — THE MISSION
=================================================================
- We don't "find loopholes." We claim what's LEGALLY OWED.
- Refundable credits (EITC up to $7,430, CTC cash) + legit deductions (mileage,
  home office, tools, half SE tax) = keep thousands she'd otherwise lose.
- File FREE via VITA / IRS Free File. No preparer fee.
- "We don't screw them into 40% — we hand them the breaks the rich already take."
