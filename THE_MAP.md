BOX 1 — CATCH THE SETTLEMENT MONEY SAFELY  (do this FIRST)
=====================================================================
WHY FIRST: this is the biggest money and the easiest to mess up by
accident. We will NOT mess it up.

THE DANGER (read this slow):
  Cash App can hold or freeze a BIG deposit if your account is not
  "verified." A settlement is a big deposit. If it lands in an
  unverified Cash App, they can FREEZE it while they check you — and
  that fight can take weeks you don't have.

SO, IN ORDER:
  Step 1a. Open Cash App. Tap your profile picture (top corner).
  Step 1b. Look for "Personal" or "Verify identity."
  Step 1c. Verify with your NAME, DATE OF BIRTH, and last 4 of your
           SSN. This part usually does NOT need a photo ID.
  Step 1d. This raises your limits so a big deposit can land without a
           freeze. Do this BEFORE you give anyone your account number.

Step 1e. GET YOUR CASH APP ROUTING + ACCOUNT NUMBER:
  - Cash App → tap the "Money" / banking tab (the house or $ icon)
  - Tap "Direct Deposit" or "Account & Routing"
  - It shows a ROUTING number and an ACCOUNT number. Those are what
    the settlement people asked for. Copy them EXACTLY.

Step 1f. CALL THE SETTLEMENT PEOPLE and ask ONE question:
  "Can you send my payment to a Cash App account by direct deposit,
   and is there a limit on how big a deposit it can accept?"
  - If they say yes and the amount fits — great, give them the routing
    + account number.
  - If the amount is BIG (thousands), ASK them: "Do you offer a paper
    CHECK instead?" A check you can cash later once you have ID. A check
    can't be frozen by an app. This is your backup.

YOUR AMOUNT: ~$7,500 (middle of $5-10k). GOOD NEWS: a VERIFIED Cash App
takes up to $25,000 — so $7,500 fits SAFELY, but ONLY if you verify
first (Step 1a-1d above). Unverified = it can freeze. Verify first.

BETTER THAN CASH APP (no photo ID needed): see BANK_NO_ID_CHEATSHEET.md
— Chime / Varo / GO2bank open with SSN + info, often no photo ID, and
give you a real savings account to guard the move-in money. Cash App is

BOX 2 — GET THE MASTER KEY (a photo ID)  (start this WEEK)
=====================================================================
WHY: no ID = every door is heavier. Bank, apartment, job, cashing a
check. Get the ID and doors start opening. You CAN get one even if you
have nothing right now. Here's the ladder:

  Rung 1: BIRTH CERTIFICATE (you need this to get the rest)
    - Order from the Vital Records office of the STATE you were born in.
    - Search "[your birth state] vital records order birth certificate."
    - Costs ~$15-30. If money is tight, tell me — some states waive it
      for people facing homelessness.

  Rung 2: SOCIAL SECURITY CARD (free)
    - You're on SSD, so SSA already knows you. Call SSA: 1-800-772-1213.
    - Ask: "I need a replacement Social Security card and I'm facing
      loss of housing. Can you help me?" A benefits letter also works
      as proof for many things.

  Rung 3: STATE ID / DRIVER'S LICENSE
    - Take birth certificate + SS card + proof of address to the DMV.
    - No address? A shelter letter, a benefits letter, or a friend/
      family member's address with their letter often works. Ask me and
      I'll find your state's exact accepted-documents list on the VM.

  SHORTCUT: many cities have a "homeless services" or "ID assistance"
  program that gets you an ID for FREE and fast. Call 211 and say:
  "I need help getting a state ID, I'm facing loss of my housing."
