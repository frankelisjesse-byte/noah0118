=================================================================
THE CORE TRUTH (all platforms)
=================================================================
Algorithms don't rank followers — they rank SIGNALS. A video with 12 followers can
outreach one with 12k if its signals are stronger. NOVA starts at zero; that's fine.
We win on signal quality (completion, shares, saves, replies), not audience size.

=================================================================
TIKTOK — primary engine
=================================================================
Ranking signals (strongest → weakest):
1. WATCH TIME / COMPLETION RATE — finish-the-video is #1. Keep videos 15-30s early.
2. REPLAYS — looping charm (her "morning you" hook) = replay bait. Design loops.
3. SHARES — strongest spread signal. "send this to someone" energy without begging.
4. SAVES — "for later" = high intent. NOVA: save-the-look, save-the-quote.
5. COMMENTS (early + meaningful) — reply to every one in her voice (memory system).
6. FOLLOWS-FROM-VIDEO — the bond makes this natural.
7. PROFILE VISITS / DWELL — her grid must be cohesive (anklet, green eyes, warmth).
EARLY VELOCITY: first 30-60 min decides distribution tier. Post when YOUR audience is
  awake (test 2-3 time slots week 1).
HOOK: first 1-3 sec. NOVA's "morning. yeah you." is a direct-address kill hook.
AUDIO: original audio (her voice) can become a trend — her charm IS the sound.
HASHTAGS: 3-5 niche (#holistichealth #morningroutine #wellness #girlnextdoor #softlife)
  over broad spam. Searchable = discovery beyond FYP.

=================================================================
INSTAGRAM — Reels + Feed + Stories
=================================================================
REELS (separate algo from Feed; pushes past followers hard):
  Signals: watch time, REPLAYS, SHARES TO DM (strongest IG signal), saves, likes.
  NOVA play: Reels = her charm cuts. "Share to a friend" framing landsnaturally.
FEED (static posts): interest + relationship + timeliness. The Day-1-10 images live
  here as a cohesive grid. Caption = the bond line, not a hard sell.
STORIES: reply-rate via stickers (poll "morning person or five-more-minutes?"). Not
  reach-distributed, but deepens the bonded few → they become sub converters.
LINK: bio = Fanvue. IG permits link-in-bio; use a bridge LP (email + Fanvue) for the
  organic-to-paid-loop retargeting.
