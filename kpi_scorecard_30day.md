# NOVA — 30-DAY KPI SCORECARD (is the business winning or stalling?)
# Pure-text. No browser needed. Update every night in 3 minutes.
# Philosophy: a number with no action is just anxiety. Each metric below tells
# you EXACTLY what to do next. Track in a notes app or a sheet — one row per day.

=================================================================
THE 6 NUMBERS THAT MATTER (track daily)
=================================================================
1. NEW FOLLOWERS / platform (TikTok, IG, X)
2. PROFILE VISITS on Fanvue (the bridge)
3. SUBS GAINED today
4. SUBS LOST today (churn)
5. PPV BUYS today ($ + count)
6. TIPS + CUSTOM $ today

Everything else is decoration. If those 6 move, you're winning.

=================================================================
WEEK 1 BENCHMARKS (what "on track" looks like)
=================================================================
These are START targets for a brand-new AI page with zero following. Adjust
up if you already have an audience; the POINT is the trend, not the absolute.

DAY 1-3 (launch blitz):
  - TikTok: 50-200 new followers/day (volume posting 3-5x/day)
  - IG: 20-80 new followers/day
  - Fanvue profile visits: 10-40/day
  - Subs: 0-3 (day 1-2 slow is NORMAL — funnel needs to fill)
  - PPV/tips: $0-15 (warm-up)

DAY 4-7:
  - TikTok: 150-500/day IF a clip catches (one viral-ish clip changes everything)
  - Fanvue visits: 30-100/day
  - Subs: 1-5 NEW/day (cumulative 3-15 by day 7)
  - PPV buys: 1-3/day, $15-45
  - Tips: $5-30

=================================================================
THE "STALL" PATTERNS — AND THE FIX FOR EACH
=================================================================
STALL A: followers climbing but ZERO Fanvue clicks
  Diagnosis: hook isn't creating curiosity, OR bio link buried.
  Fix NOW:
   - Every post must END with a curiosity line + "link in bio for the real me."
   - Put Fanvue link in bio + a Story highlight pinned "uncensored 👇".
   - Post one "behind the curtain" clip: "this is the stuff i can't post here."
  Red line: if day 7 and <10 profile visits total -> hook problem, rebuild captions.

STALL B: clicks but NO subs
  Diagnosis: preview content weak, or price/offer unclear.
  Fix NOW:
   - Free wall post MUST tease the paid value (show barefoot collection, hint heat).
   - Drop intro price to $9.99 and SAY it ("first 50 subs locked at $9.99").
   - Welcome DM must land within minutes of subscribe (auto-reply on).
  Red line: >100 visits, 0 subs by day 10 -> preview assets or bio failing.

STALL C: subs but high churn (lose as fast as you gain)
  Diagnosis: retention content missing. They subscribed on promise, got silence.
  Fix NOW:
   - Daily "good morning" post minimum.
   - DM at least 5 existing subs/day with a real question (use dm_scripts.md).
   - Drop a PPV they didn't pay for as a "thanks for staying" gift to 1 loyal sub.
  Red line: churn > 30% weekly -> you're renting subs, not keeping them.

STALL D: subs steady but $ flat (no PPV/tips)
  Diagnosis: not asking. Heat tier makes its money ABOVE the sub.
  Fix NOW:
   - 3-4 PPV drops/week with teaser caption ("full version behind the lock 🔓").
   - Tip menu live ($5/$10/$25 buttons) + a "fund my new set" post.
   - Custom request CTA once/week ("tell me your fantasy, i'll price it").
  Red line: subs >10 and PPV $0 for a week -> you're leaving 60% of revenue on table.

=================================================================
THE WIN PATTERNS — DO MORE OF THIS
=================================================================
WIN 1: one clip 5x your usual views -> IMMEDIATELY post 5 more like it today.
  (format, audio, hook, outfit — clone the winner, don't "get creative.")
WIN 2: a caption gets 20+ comments -> that quirk/question is GOLD. Make it a series.
WIN 3: a PPV drops and 40%+ of subs buy -> that angle is your money content.
  Produce a bundle of 3-5 similar, release across 2 weeks.
WIN 4: a follower DMs first -> they're warm. Welcome script + soft upsell.

=================================================================
WEEKLY REVIEW RITUAL (every Sunday, 10 min)
=================================================================
1. Pull the week's 6 numbers into one row.
2. Rank all posts by engagement. Top 3 = your formula. Bottom 3 = kill.
3. Ask: which STALL pattern am I in? Apply the fix above.
4. Set next week's ONE test: new hook style / new post time / new PPV angle.
5. Batch next week's images in one sitting (prompts.md) so posting stays easy.

=================================================================
THE ONLY 3 NUMBERS THAT DECIDE "KEEP GOING"
=================================================================
At day 30, you are WINNING if:
  - 200+ cumulative followers on at least one platform, OR
  - 15+ paid subs, OR
  - $200+ total revenue (sub + PPV + tips + custom)
You are STALLING (pivot, don't quit) if:
  - <50 followers AND 0 subs AND $0 -> the hook/offer isn't landing; rebuild
    public content engine before spending more time on Fanvue side.
  - subs but 0 revenue beyond sub -> PPV/tips engine not switched on (STALL D).
  - revenue but churning -> retention fix (STALL C) before scaling traffic.

Note: $200 in month 1 from zero is a WORKING funnel. The ramp is month 2-3.
Don't judge a content business on week-1 numbers — judge the TREND.
