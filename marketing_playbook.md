# MARKETING + BUSINESS PLAYBOOK — NOVA

NOTE: Live web research was unavailable when this was written (tool broken on device).
The strategy below is built from established knowledge of the Fanvue/OnlyFans + social
funnel. Platform rules change constantly — verify current terms yourself before launch.

=====================================================================
PART 1 — WHERE SHE LIVES (platform strategy)
=====================================================================
PRIMARY PAID HOME: Fanvue (not OnlyFans) for an AI model.
  - Fanvue explicitly allows AI-generated creators and is built for this.
  - OnlyFans requires ID verification of a REAL person and has tightened rules;
    pure-AI accounts there are risky and may be banned. Use Fanvue as the base.
  - You CAN still run an OnlyFans later with a real-person verification, but for a
    pure-AI character, Fanvue (or similar: Fanvue, Pixelfed-adjacent, My.Club) is safer.
  - Decide: Fanvue = primary. Keep OF as a possible future "real-person" expansion.

TRAFFIC SOURCES (free, where you drive followers from):
  - TikTok: #1 reach engine. Short body/wellness clips, trending audio, hook in 1 sec.
  - Instagram Reels: second reach engine, also where the "pretty aesthetic" lands.
  - X/Twitter: where the "heat" funnel lives openly. Post teases, link in bio.
  - Reddit: niche subs (fitness, holistic, specific interest communities) for targeted reach.
  - Threads: bonus reach, low effort.

=====================================================================
PART 2 — THE FUNNEL (how a stranger becomes $)
=====================================================================
1. STRANGER sees a TikTok/Reel (real wellness value + flirty hook).
2. Follows -> sees Stories/posts planting curiosity ("the uncensored me lives elsewhere").
3. Bio link -> Fanvue profile (free preview posts + paid tiers).
4. Converts to $ -> subscription, then PPV (pay-per-view) custom + tips.
5. RETENTION -> daily DMs, polls, "good morning" messages. The relationship = the retention.

Money layers (in order of reliability):
  A. Monthly subscription ($9.99–$19.99/mo is the sweet spot to start).
  B. PPV locked content (the "heat" — priced $5–$30 per clip/set).
  C. Custom requests (premium, $50–$200+).
  D. Tips / "send me a coffee" (low friction, frequent).
  E. Bundles / multi-month discounts to lock retention.

=====================================================================
PART 3 — CONTENT THAT SELLS (what to actually post)
=====================================================================
PUBLIC (TikTok/IG) — value first, flirt second:
  - "Get ready with me" wellness routines (hair, skincare, pre-workout).
  - Workout clips: glute/ab/leg focus, body shots, gym mirror, stretch flow.
  - Recipe/cooking (green smoothie, meal prep) — aspirational lifestyle.
  - "Soft life / holistic" aesthetic B-roll (plants, sun, calm).
  - Flirty caption + eye contact = the curiosity hook. NEVER explicit publicly.
  - Post 2–4x/day on TikTok (volume wins), 1 Reel + 1–2 static/day on IG.

PAID (Fanvue) — the heat:
  - Bolder visuals (lingerie/seductive, generated separately, NOT on public accts).
  - Daily "good morning" style intimate posts.
  - PPV drops 3–4x/week.
  - Respond to DMs like a real girlfriend who's busy but cares.

VIDEO: I cannot generate video in this environment. Workaround:
  - Use the generated images as "talking photo" / parallax via apps:
    * CapCut / D-ID / Hedra / Luma Dream Machine to animate stills into video.
    * Or compile image slideshows with trending audio on CapCut (works, easy, free).
  - For real motion later: runway/cinematic AI video (Runway, Kling, Luma) on a PC.

=====================================================================
PART 4 — BUSINESS MANAGEMENT (treat it like a company)
=====================================================================
Daily metrics to watch (relentless = she tracks these every day):
  - New followers (per platform)
  - Click-throughs to Fanvue
  - Sub conversions (% of profile visitors who pay)
  - Churn (% who cancel) — fight this with retention content
  - PPV buy rate
  - Top 3 performing posts -> make 10 more like them

Weekly:
  - Double down on what converted. Kill what didn't.
  - Batch-create next week's images in one session (efficiency).
  - Test one new hook/format.

Tools (mostly free / cheap):
  - Scheduling: Later, Metricool, or Buffer (free tiers).
  - Image gen: this assistant (FAL backend) — batch, then schedule.
  - Caption writing: this assistant (prompts.md formulas).
  - Analytics: platform native + a sheet you update daily.
  - Payment: Fanvue handles processing; you get paid out weekly/monthly.

Money discipline (critical with a house at risk):
  - Separate business account. Pay yourself a fixed amount; reinvest the rest into
    ads + better tools until stable.
  - Once income covers the mortgage, that's the floor — don't scale lifestyle.
  - Taxes: this is self-employment income. Set aside ~25–30% for taxes.

PART 6 — REALISTIC TIMELINE (so you don't quit too early)
=====================================================================
  - Week 1–2: build accounts, post daily, expect ~$0–$50. Keep going.
  - Week 3–4: first consistent subs, maybe $100–$500 if posting hard.
  - Month 2–3: if relentless, $1k–$5k/mo is achievable for a strong AI character.
  - Month 4–6: $5k–$20k/mo possible with paid traffic + retention.
