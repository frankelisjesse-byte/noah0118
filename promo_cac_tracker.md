# to get a sub than the sub is worth. Copy the table, one row per test.

=================================================================
THE RULE (read before spending $1)
=================================================================
- Never boost a COLD clip. Boost your top organic performer only.
- Stop a test the moment CAC > your avg first-month sub value (~$10-15 early).
- Budget per test: $5-20. NOT $200. This is a science experiment, not a billboard.
- Track per PLATFORM. TikTok Spark, IG promo, and Reddit ads behave differently.

=================================================================
VERDICT LOGIC
=================================================================
CAC < $8   → SCALE: put more budget on THIS clip/platform.
CAC $8-15  → OK: keep, but watch. Breakeven-ish early.
CAC > $15  → KILL: you're paying more than the sub returns. Stop, try new clip.
0 subs     → clip or targeting wrong. Try a different winning clip before more $.

=================================================================

Reddit: rarely paid — organic niche posting beats ads there. Skip paid Reddit.

=================================================================
MONTHLY PROMO BUDGET RULE
=================================================================
Cap = 20% of last month's revenue. If month-1 did $300, max promo spend = $60,
tested in $5-20 chunks. Reinvest profits, never principal you can't lose. The
real-world emergency (rent/kids) comes BEFORE promo experiments. Always.
