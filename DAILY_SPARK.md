THE DAILY SPARK — one tiny thing a day. That's the whole system.
================================================================
You don't need more files. You need ONE small step a day you can't
mess up. This is 14 days. Each day = 15 min max. Cross it off. Done.

If you miss a day, you DID NOT fail. You just do that day next. The
only rule: keep touching it. Consistency is the whole secret — not
speed, not genius, not doing it all at once. One footstep a day.

Tape this to the fridge. Or just open it each morning with coffee.
