# NOVA — DM CONVERSATION SCRIPTS (retention + tips engine)
# Voice: lowercase, intimate, asks questions, never desperate. She's the
# girlfriend who's busy but CARES. Goal: turn a sub into a loyal regular who
# tips. Never hard-sell — make them WANT to.

=================================================================
RULE 1 — THE 24H REPLY RULE
=================================================================
Reply within 24h always. A fan who gets a real reply feels chosen. Chosen fans
don't churn. Busy day? Send the 0.3s voice-note hum. It counts.

=================================================================
RULE 2 — ASK, DON'T PERFORM
=================================================================
Every reply ends by turning the question back on them. "what'd YOU come for?"
"you into the workouts or the chaos?" People tip the girl who's curious about
THEM, not the one who performs.

=================================================================
RULE 3 — THE SOFT SELL (never broke)
=================================================================
Don't say "buy my PPV." Say "dropped something i think you'll like, it's up."
Make the paid thing feel like a gift chosen FOR them.

=================================================================
SCRIPT A — NEW SUB WELCOME (auto + personal touch)
=================================================================
auto: "hi baby, you made it 😏 welcome to the dangerous side. tell me what you
came for and i'll show you where to look. 🤍"
then manually within 24h: "ok real talk — what's your type, the wellness chaos
or the heat? i'll curate your feed 😘"  (this segments them for future sells)

=================================================================
SCRIPT B — TIP TRIGGER (when they comment but haven't tipped)
=================================================================
"you're always here and i notice 🤍 you'd LOVE the drop from tuesday, it's still
up if you wanna see the uncut version 😏"  (soft, specific, no pressure)

=================================================================
SCRIPT C — LOYAL SUB (week 2+, no churn risk)
=================================================================
"ok you're officially my favorite regular and i'm not apologizing. picked
something out for you this week — check the wall. don't tell the others 🤫"

=================================================================
SCRIPT D — WIN-BACK (sub goes quiet 5+ days)
=================================================================
"the rocks on my windowsill said someone's been MIA 👀 you good? dropped a
little something for the loyal ones, thought of you. 🤍"

=================================================================
SCRIPT E — REQUEST/CUSTOM (high value)
=================================================================
"you want something specific? tell me. i do customs — start at $60, scales with
how dangerous you're thinking 😏 what's the vibe?"

=================================================================
SCRIPT F — FEET NICHE CROSS-SELL
=================================================================
"you ever notice my ankles/feet in the workout clips? i posts the close-ups on
the wall — soft, pampered, just for the ones who look 👣 thought you'd want in."

=================================================================
THE TIER LADDER (in conversation)
=================================================================
free follower → "you'd like the uncut version on my page 😏" (sub)
sub → "the tuesday drop is the kind of thing you tip for 🤍" (PPV)
PPV buyer → "loyal ones get first dibs + a free surprise monthly 🤫" (retain)
loyal → custom request (highest $)

Every fan climbs ONE rung per convo. Never skip rungs (don't hard-sell a new
sub a $200 custom). Meet them where they are.
