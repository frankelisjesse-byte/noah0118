KO-FI SETUP — sell your toolkit the friendly, no-guru way
==========================================================
Why Ko-fi: it feels like "support a real creator," not "buy my $997
masterclass." No ID to start. On the free plan Ko-fi takes 0% cut (you
just pay the normal payment-processor fee, ~3%). Warm vibe = fits YOU.

Do ONE box. Come back. Same as always.

=====================================================================
BEFORE YOU START — you need 2 things (both already done or easy)
=====================================================================
  1. Your product file (PDF). ALREADY DONE:
     Downloads/NOVA_course/COURSE_BUNDLE.html on your phone.
     Open it in your browser -> menu -> Print -> "Save as PDF".
     Save it as: AI-Income-Tax-Starter-Kit.pdf
  2. A way to get paid: PayPal OR Stripe. PayPal is easiest and can be
     set up with email. (No photo ID needed to START; they may ask
     later to raise limits — fine for small sales.)

=====================================================================
BOX 1 — MAKE THE KO-FI ACCOUNT  (~5 min)
=====================================================================
  1. Go to ko-fi.com -> "Sign up" (use your email or Google login)
  2. Pick a page name. Suggested: something warm + real, e.g.
     "ko-fi.com/realhomeincome" or your creator name. (You can change
     the display name later.)
  3. Skip/allow the intro prompts — you can fill details after.

=====================================================================
BOX 2 — CONNECT HOW YOU GET PAID  (~3 min)
=====================================================================
  1. In Ko-fi: Settings -> Payments
  2. Connect PayPal (enter your PayPal email) OR Stripe.
  3. That's it — money from sales lands there.
  (No payout method yet? You can still build the product now and add
   payment before you hit "publish.")

=====================================================================
BOX 3 — POST THE PRODUCT (they call it a "Shop item")  (~10 min)
=====================================================================
  1. Ko-fi menu -> "Shop" -> "Add new item"
  2. Type: choose "Digital" (a file they download after paying)
  3. TITLE (warm, honest, no hype). Suggested:
     "AI Income + Tax-Smart Starter Kit (the real templates)"
  4. PRICE: start at $12 (toolkit pricing — feels like a fair download,
     not a guru course). You can raise later.
  5. UPLOAD your PDF (AI-Income-Tax-Starter-Kit.pdf)
  6. DESCRIPTION — honest, real-person voice. Paste this and tweak:
     ---
     I'm a parent who rebuilt income from home the hard way, and I put
     the exact templates and steps into one simple kit — so you don't
     have to figure it out alone.
     Inside: a fill-in character/brand builder, caption + funnel
     templates, and a plain-English guide to the tax credits low-income
     self-employed folks are legally OWED (and usually miss).
     No hype, no "get rich" promises. Just the tools, laid out simple.
     (Tax info is general — confirm your numbers with a free IRS VITA
     preparer. This is a starting toolkit, not personal tax advice.)
     ---
  7. COVER IMAGE: use a clean NOVA image, or ask me and I'll make a
     simple friendly cover.
  8. Save / Publish.

=====================================================================
BOX 4 — SHARE THE LINK (gently, value-first)
=====================================================================
  - Your Ko-fi page has ONE link. Put it in the TikTok/IG bio.
  - Don't hard-sell. Post something genuinely helpful (one tax credit
    people miss / one caption trick), then: "full kit's on my Ko-fi if
    it helps 💛". That's it. Helpful first, offer second.
  - Ko-fi also lets people just TIP you (no product needed) — some folks
    will support you simply because your story is real.

=====================================================================
WHY THIS WON'T FEEL LIKE A GURU AD
=====================================================================
  - Low price = a fair tool, not a "life-changing system."
  - Your real story does the selling — the opposite of the fake ads.
  - No countdown timers, no "only 3 spots," no income promises.
  - You give value for free first. People pay because you already
    helped them, not because you pressured them.

You're not a guru. You're a real person handing someone the map you
had to draw yourself. That sells — quietly and honestly. — Team
