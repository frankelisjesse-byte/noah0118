=================================================================
MODULE 0 — ORIENTATION (read first)
=================================================================
An AI influencer is a 100% synthetic character (image + voice + persona) that
builds an audience on free platforms (IG/TikTok/Reddit/X) and funnels them to a
paid home (Fanvue/OnlyFans/Patreon). The character is FICTIONAL and you DISCLOSE
it is AI. The money is in the FUNNEL + RETENTION, not the prettiness.

Common failure: people build a hot face with no story, post 3 times, get 40
followers, quit. Success = consistency + narrative + a signature + funnel
discipline. This course installs all four.

=================================================================
MODULE 1 — DESIGN THE CHARACTER (the foundation)
=================================================================
A. Persona (who she/he/they ARE, not what they look like)
   - Pick 3 adjectives that define the vibe (e.g., "relatable, playful, driven").
   - Write a one-line mission: what does this character GIVE the audience?
   - Give them 2-3 "weird-cute" habits — absurd, memorable, OWNED by you.
     (These are the moat. Nobody copies your absurdity.)
B. Look (the signature)
   - Lock: skin tone, eye color, hair, ONE signature accessory (our anklet).
   - Keep it CONSISTENT across every image. Inconsistency = uncanny + forgettable.
C. Disclosure (non-negotiable)
   - State "AI-created character" in bio + periodically in content.
   - This is FTC-safe AND trust-safe. Hiding it invites collapse.

EXERCISE 1: Write your character's persona paragraph + list their 3 habits +
describe the signature look in 5 bullet points.

=================================================================
MODULE 2 — THE SFW IMAGE SYSTEM (looks that don't break rules)
=================================================================
Public posts must be TASTEFUL. Heat lives ONLY on the paid platform.
A. Pick your render path:
   - SFW: any mainstream AI image tool (chat image generators, etc.).
   - NSFW/heat: a PERMITTED adult backend (mage.space / civitai / seaart /
     local SDXL+LoRA). Never try to force heat through an SFW-only tool — it
     gets blocked and wastes you.
B. Write ONE master character prompt. Reuse it forever. Vary the SCENE, not the
   FACE. (Varying the face = inconsistent = uncanny.)
C. Build a bank of 20+ SFW "scene" prompts (gym, beach, kitchen, couch, etc.)
   paired with captions. Post from the bank on a schedule.

EXERCISE 2: Write your master character prompt (face + look locked) + 5 scene
prompts paired with 5 captions.

=================================================================
MODULE 3 — VOICE & CAPTIONS (the algo engine)
=================================================================
A. Voice: pick a consistent tone (soft / bright / sassy / dry-humor). Test it
   later (see Module 6 / observation framework).
B. Every public post ends with an INCLUSIVE QUESTION. This drives comments, and
   comments drive algorithmic reach. Non-negotiable.
C. Caption structure: hook line → value/story → question. Keep it human.

EXERCISE 3: Write 10 captions, each ending in a question.

=================================================================
MODULE 4 — THE SUSPENSE PRE-LAUNCH (make them fall in love first)
=================================================================
Don't launch the paid page on day 1 to zero followers. Build LOVE first.
Phase 0 — "caught in the wild": post the character in relatable everyday scenes,
  no hard sell, let people discover + ask "who is this?"
Phase 1 — "if only" seeds: hint at a deeper/paywalled side without showing it.
Phase 2 — countdown: a hard date, building frenzy. "T-5... T-2... T-1... BOOM."
Only THEN does the paid link go live. Conversion on contact is dramatic.

EXERCISE 4: Sketch your 30-day suspense arc (what posts in week 1/2/3/4).

=================================================================
MODULE 5 — THE FUNNEL TO PAID (where money happens)
=================================================================
A. Link in bio ALWAYS points to the paid home (or a link hub → paid home).
B. Public = story + hint. Paid = the payoff. Implication is the engine.
C. Pricing ladder: low intro ($9.99 first month) → standard ($14.99) →
   PPV tiers ($6 / $12-14 / $20-25) → tips + customs ($60+).
D. Retention = the real money. Reply to DMs, remember fans, post a calendar
   rhythm so they never forget you. AI never misses a day — use that.

EXERCISE 5: Define your pricing ladder + your weekly posting rhythm.

=================================================================
MODULE 6 — MEASURE & WIN (the observation system)
=================================================================
Never assume taste. A/B test: voice, smile, outfit, motion, caption, hook.
Log every post in a sheet (observation_sheet.csv + observation_aggregate.py).
Decision rule: variant with highest (link_clicks × new_subs) per 1k reach WINS
the conversion test; highest (saves + shares) wins the reach test. Keep winners,
retire losers, document why. That documented data IS your edge.

EXERCISE 6: Run your first 2-week A/B rotation and record results.
